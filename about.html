About SecurePass

SecurePass is a lightweight, client-side password strength tool designed to help you make better password decisions. All processing is done locally in your browser.



Built with

  • HTML, CSS, and vanilla JavaScript
  • Client-side password strength evaluation
  • No data is sent to any server

Privacy First

SecurePass operates on a Zero-Knowledge principle. Your passwords are never transmitted to our servers or stored in our database. The evaluation happens entirely within your browser's memory and is cleared as soon as you close the tab.

How it works

The password checker looks for length, variety of character types, and common weak patterns. Strong passwords generally include:

  • At least 12 characters
  • A mix of letters, numbers, and symbols
  • No repeated or predictable patterns (e.g. "1234" or "password")

Why SecurePass?

With billions of credentials leaked every year, weak passwords are the #1 entry point for cyberattacks. This tool aims to educate users on creating "Entropy-rich" passwords that are resistant to Brute Force and Dictionary attacks.
